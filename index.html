How It Works

Our picture book generator uses OpenAI's GPT-3.5 to understand your prompt and create a story based on it. Dall-E is then used to generate images that correspond to the story. The result is a unique and personalized picture book for you to enjoy.

Examples

Prompt: A magical adventure with talking animals

Generated Book: "The Enchanted Forest Adventure: A Tale of Friendship and Magic"

Prompt: A child's journey through space

Generated Book: "Astronaut Alex: Exploring the Wonders of the Universe"
